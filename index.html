Computer Vision-Based Interactive Basketball Training Platform

Point Guard

According to SLBF over 10,000 members play basketball in Sri Lanka.

Traditional basketball training relies on manual observation, limiting the accuracy of skill development.

Integrating computer vision, machine learning, and analytics overcomes traditional training limits.

Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

Computer vision algorithms have become increasingly accurate and reliable in tracking the trajectory and precision of basketball shots, providing valuable insights into player performance. Hoop-tracking systems offer real-time feedback during training sessions, enabling athletes to correct their form instantly and optimize shooting performance. Incorporating these systems into training programs significantly enhances skill development by delivering personalized feedback. Customizing workout plans for individual players relies on advanced algorithms that assess body size, inputs, and composition, tailoring strength training to their needs while promoting injury prevention and muscle group development. Movement-tracking systems provide real-time feedback, helping beginners assess and correct their movements effectively, while self-development practices empower players with limited knowledge of player movement to improve their skills. Progress tracking and performance analytics identify individual strengths and weaknesses, facilitating targeted training interventions. Additionally, machine learning and predictive analytics enable the forecasting of future performance, offering deeper insights into areas requiring improvement and helping athletes reach their full potential through data-driven strategies.

Proposed Solution

The research solution focuses on developing a comprehensive basketball training application that enhances player performance through advanced analytics and personalized recommendations. By incorporating user inputs collected via surveys, the app can provide tailored feedback on shooting mechanics, defense techniques, and overall performance. The system integrates body analysis and real-time movement tracking to design strength training programs that cater to individual needs, ensuring precise and customized workout plans. Utilizing machine learning algorithms, the app predicts player movements more accurately and offers personalized suggestions to improve gameplay. Players can engage in practice sessions that adapt based on performance analytics, enabling continuous progress tracking and refinement of skills. With a user-friendly interface, the application ensures accessibility while empowering players to learn, practice, and achieve their goals in scoring, dribbling, and defending.

Research Objectives

Track the player's Dribbling movement and personalized suggestion

Beginner players can learn the basics of dribbling through our platform, gaining an understanding of how to control the ball effectively. They can also explore common mistakes made by professional players, such as improper hand positioning or lack of focus, helping them recognize and avoid these errors early on. Our tutorials provide step-by-step guidance on how to dribble correctly for both scoring and defending, ensuring players develop proper techniques to maintain ball control under pressure, create scoring opportunities, and strengthen their defensive skills.

Enhance Hoop Tracking Accuracy

Our platform provides players with precise and accurate feedback on their shooting mechanics and performance, helping them identify areas for improvement and refine their skills. To further enhance hoop tracking accuracy, we develop advanced methods for precise shot trajectory tracking, ensuring reliable data on shot angles, release points, and scoring patterns. This detailed analysis enables players to make informed adjustments and consistently improve their shooting performance.

Design and Implementation of Strength Training Programs with User Input, Body Analysis, and Customized Workout Plans

We collect user inputs through surveys to understand each player’s fitness goals and preferences, ensuring personalized training experiences. With this data, we design and implement strength training programs that integrate body analysis, including factors like height, weight, and body composition. This approach allows us to create customized workout plans tailored to the specific needs of each player, focusing on injury prevention, muscle development, and overall performance improvement. As players progress, the platform adjusts these plans based on their feedback, ensuring continuous optimization and alignment with their evolving goals.

Progress tracking and Performance analytics

Our platform gathers data from hoop tracking, movement tracking, and strength training to provide a comprehensive view of player performance. This data enables more accurate player performance predictions, helping players and coaches identify areas for improvement. Based on these insights, the system suggests personalized practice sessions tailored to individual needs, ensuring targeted skill development. Additionally, progress tracking and performance analytics offer real-time feedback on strengths and weaknesses, allowing players to monitor their growth and make data-driven adjustments to their training routines.
